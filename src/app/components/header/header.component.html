Game Rules

Components:

  • Game Board
  • Question Cards
  • Know-it-All Donkey
  • Minimum 2 Players
  • Maximum 4 Players

 

Objective of the game:

The objective is to be the first player to land on the arrival square located in the hindquarters of the central donkey.

 

Game Rules:

  • Player 1 rolls the die and moves their token according to the number that comes up.
  • Pass the turn to the next player.
  • Spaces on the board:
  • KNOW-IT-ALL DONKEY: This is a bonus question. If a player lands on this square after rolling the die, the Reader selects and reads a bonus question from the

KNOW-IT-ALL DONKEY category (red). Only the Player who land on the KNOW-IT-ALL DONKEY square can attempt to answer the question. If the player answers correctly, they roll the movement die and advance again. If the answer is incorrect, then pass the turn to the next player.

  • DONKEY'S KICK: If a player lands on a DONKEY'S KICK square, move their pawn three squares back.

 

Winning the game:

The first player to reach the ARRIVAL square in the hindquarters of the donkey is declared the winner.
